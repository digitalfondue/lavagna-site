Why support Lavagna

Lavagna is a ongoing open-source effort, by donating you ensure that the project is kept alive.

Currently, every donation will go towards paying for basic expenses, such as domains and development servers. In the future, we are looking to use any form of revenue to cover for the developers time.

We plan to release some sort of financial statement every three months, describing what your money did achieve.
